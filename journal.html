Monday, April 15, 2013

I can't put into words how EXCITED I am that classes have started and are just as awesome as I imagined!

Web design is something I've been interested in since the glory days of Xanga and MySpace when I would spend many a night forgoing my pre-algebra for making flashy profiles full of gifs (yes, they existed back then), busy backdrops, and angst-ridden quotations.

And to be honest, as the 2-year anniversary of my college graduation draws closer and closer, I really simply miss being in school.

I absolutely thrive in stressful situations, which is why I like to keep my plate full. The most important thing you must know about me, is that I never (and I mean never) have just one job.

I'm currently working in social media at the Chicago History Museum, and on the side I run my own jewelry business Rusted Revolution and write on the reg for Choose Chicago's Chicago Like a Local blog.

Luckily, my life has slowed down a minuscule fraction of a bit, so I'm filling up all that spare time with HTML/CSS and Visual Design classes.

I can't wait to start cramming my brain with information and letting all my creative juices flow.

And of course, I hope to make some new friends along the way. So hi.
